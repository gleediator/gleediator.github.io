- How can you help -

Super cool that you care! I am actively looking for LGBT stories that I can share on the site. There's no limitation, you can be an artist, writer, musician, developer, broker, makeup artist, painter, firefighter, waitress, barista, god-am-losing-my-breath, any-gay-one!

I will put up a video soon as an example.

I prefer a video recording with these specific subjects:

  • Please tell me a a bit about yourself(name, age, occupation, location).
  • When and how did you realize that you're not straight?
  • Do you still remember your first coming out story ever?
  • Does your sexual preference affect your professional environtment?
  • *optional* Message you wanna share to both struggling LGBT youth & public in Indonesia.

If you're ready, please ping me here. Thank you so so much in advance!
Note: All interviews will be translated to Indonesian (either with subtitle or text - will find the best solution).

Interested to help? You can Donate your stories/skills ~ I am looking for great-motivating stories!
